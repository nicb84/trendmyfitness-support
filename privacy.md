# Privacy Policy — TrendMyFitness

*Last updated: May 12, 2025*

## Overview
TrendMyFitness ("the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection
TrendMyFitness does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data generated by the app is stored locally on your device only.

## HealthKit
TrendMyFitness accesses Apple HealthKit to read and save your workout and fitness data. This data never leaves your device and is never shared with third parties. TrendMyFitness does not use HealthKit data for advertising or other data-mining purposes.

## Third-Party Services
TrendMyFitness does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Deletion
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app.
